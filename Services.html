Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation solutions tailored to your needs

Comprehensive Care for Every Need

From orthopedic rehabilitation to neurological recovery, our expert team provides personalized, evidence-based treatment plans designed to restore your health and mobility.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Expert care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions with specialized rehabilitation programs.

  • Post-surgical Rehabilitation (TKR, ACL, THR)
  • Sports Injury Recovery
  • Arthritis Management
  • Fracture Rehabilitation
  • Joint Replacement Recovery

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized programs for neurological conditions focusing on functional recovery.

  • Stroke Recovery & Rehabilitation
  • Parkinson's Disease Management
  • Spinal Cord Injury Rehab
  • Balance & Coordination Training
  • Neuroplasticity-Based Therapy

Sports Injury & Recovery

Athlete-focused rehabilitation to get you back in the game stronger and safer.

  • ACL Reconstruction Rehab
  • Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Injuries
  • Ankle Sprains & Strains
  • Performance Enhancement
  • Injury Prevention Programs

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Specialized care for women through pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond.

  • Antenatal & Postnatal Care
  • Pelvic Floor Rehabilitation
  • Pregnancy-Related Back Pain
  • Diastasis Recti Treatment
  • Postpartum Recovery Programs

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, playful therapy to help children develop motor skills and overcome challenges.

  • Developmental Delays
  • Cerebral Palsy Management
  • Muscular Dystrophy Care
  • Coordination & Balance Training
  • Posture Correction for Kids

Home Care Physiotherapy

Professional physiotherapy services delivered at your home across South India.

  • Post-Surgical Home Care
  • Elderly & Geriatric Care
  • Mobility & ADL Training
  • Pain Management at Home
  • Available in Telangana, AP, Bangalore & TN
